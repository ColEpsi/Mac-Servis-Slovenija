Mac computer repair

We are the leading slovenian company in out of warranty Macbook Pro, Macbook Air, Mac mini, iMac and Mac Pro repair.

Component upgrades

You may have purchased your computer thinking you have enough storage or RAM for the future but it later turns out that is not the case. Apple does not do computer upgrades so this is where we step in. We offer cost efficient longterm upgrades depending on the type of your Apple computer.

Motherboard repair

It can happen to any of us. May be a drop, liquid damage or a random failure, but we are faced with a dead computer. Authorised service providers are required to replace the whole motherboard, making the repair pricy and losing all your data. We offer component level repair, preserving your data and lowering the price of the repair.
 

Cleaning

Minor spills can if acted quickly be dried out without the need for motherboard repair. We offer comprehensive thorough cleaning of liquid in your keyboard or internals, aswell as removing dust from internals of your Mac.

We are using the latest equipment and techniques which enable us to do high quality longterm repair of your devices.

Repair timeline

Drop off

Before we take you device in, we do a preliminary expection of your device. We also like to ask about the device history and any details that might lead to a faster solution.

Diagnostics

Our technicians will take an in depth look at your device, pinpointing the issue and finding the optimal longterm solution.

Consulting

After a successful diagnostic of the problem we will contact you and share our findings along with the associated costs. It is then up to you to decide if you think the repair is viable and give the go ahead. If not you can have the device returned to you in its original state with no cost.   

Repair

Our technicians will use utmost care in fixing your device, using only OEM parts or fixing your original parts when possible.

Device pick up

After the repair we contact you about the success of the repair and arrange the pick up.
