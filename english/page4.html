iOS device repair

Your mobile devices store an immense amount of data and memories, which can be lost the instant you drop or drown your device.

For that reason we highly recommend keeping a timely backup of all your devices, either through iCloud, Mac or other backup services. If your iPhone/iPad is already broken, not responding to touch, rebooting or completely dead, we offer comprehensive services that will save your data and device if possible.

Screen replacement

We offer quality aftermarket screen replacements for all iPhone, iPad devices using OEM replacement screens that are made in the same factories as the originals.

2

Battery replacement   

Your worn out and malfunctioning battery will be replaced with a quality OEM unit that will once again give you the all day battery life you need.

General and logic board repair
