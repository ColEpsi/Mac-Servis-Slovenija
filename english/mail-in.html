Return shipping


We pack the device in the packaging in which it came, and send it back to you free of charge and with tracking.

Shipping recommendations 

1

Use original packaging

Whenever possible, we recommend that you use the original packaging that came with your device for shipping. If you do not have it anymore, use a box large enough and make sure that your device does not touch the box directly. To do this, either use dedicated air bubble wrap or crumpled newspaper.

2

Make sure things don’t move

Unfortunately, we are unable to influence the circumstances in which your device will find itself, so we recommend that you shake the entire package before shipping and make sure that there are no moving objects in it. In case there are, you can use newspaper and cram it into the empty spaces.

3

Send only your defective device

Please do not include items that do not require repair in the shipment. By this we mean mainly various device chargers and covers that can be misplaced during repair.
